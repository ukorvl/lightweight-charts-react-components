---
title: Time Scale
description: Configure visible ranges, subscribe to scale events, and nest time-scale
  helpers under a chart.
---

`TimeScale` is the wrapper entry point for upstream time scale options and range-change
events. Nest it inside a chart when you want to control the visible range declaratively or
subscribe to time-scale updates.

<CodeExample path="time-scale.tsx" title="Time scale options and fit-content" />

The wrapper exposes thin types such as `TimeScaleProps` and `TimeScaleApiRef`, while the
large option surface stays owned by Lightweight Charts itself.

See the full
[Scales sample](https://github.com/ukorvl/lightweight-charts-react-components/tree/main/examples/src/samples/Scales)
for more event-driven scale usage.
