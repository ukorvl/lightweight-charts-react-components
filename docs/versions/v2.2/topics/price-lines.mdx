---
title: Price Lines
description: Attach declarative horizontal price lines to any series.
---

`PriceLine` is nested inside a series and manages the lifecycle of the matching upstream
price line plugin for you. It is a good fit for alerts, targets, thresholds, and other
reference levels that should move with the series.

<CodeExample path="price-line.tsx" title="Series with a price line" />

The component keeps the wrapper-specific surface small: you provide the target `price`
and pass upstream line styling through `options`.

See the full
[Price lines sample](https://github.com/ukorvl/lightweight-charts-react-components/tree/main/examples/src/samples/PriceLines)
for a more interactive example.
