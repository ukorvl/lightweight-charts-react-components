title: Series
description: Use built-in series components and shared series props without dropping down
  to imperative chart code.
---

Series components wrap the matching Lightweight Charts series constructors and stay close
to the upstream API. The wrapper keeps the props thin: you mostly pass `data`, `options`,
and a few wrapper-specific controls such as `reactive`, `seriesOrder`, and
`alwaysReplaceData`.

When you need multiple panes, multiple series types, or price and volume overlays, you
can compose them as regular React children.
